"Skyfire Rising" is a science fiction novel written by Ramesh Kulkarni. 
It was published in 2023 by Horizon Press. 
The story is set in a future where India builds the first space elevator in Sriharikota. 
The novel won the 2024 National Sci-Fi Award for Best Original Work. 
A sequel, titled "Skyfire Eternal," is planned for release in 2026.

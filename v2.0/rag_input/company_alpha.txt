Company Alpha is a startup based in Pune, India. 
It was founded in 2022 by Priya Nair and focuses on building eco-friendly packaging solutions using sugarcane fibers. 
In 2024, Company Alpha launched its flagship product called BioPack Lite, which is fully biodegradable within 60 days. 
The company received $3 million in Series A funding from GreenVentures Capital in 2025.
